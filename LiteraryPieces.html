Epic (Biag ni Lam-ang)

"Biag ni Lam-ang," is an epic from the Philippines, specifically from the Ilocano region. It narrates the extraordinary life of Lam-ang, a legendary hero known for his incredible strength and magical abilities. The story unfolds with Lam-ang's birth, marked by miraculous events, and follows his adventures, including his quest for his missing father and his encounters with mythical creatures. Filled with elements of folklore, romance, and heroism, the epic offers insights into Filipino culture and tradition.
